Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 7, 2026

ScoreTalk (the "App") values your privacy and protects personal information in accordance with applicable laws.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects the following information through direct user input:

  • Class (course) names
  • Student names and student IDs
  • Score column names and student scores
  • Voice data (temporarily processed during score entry)

2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Recording and managing grades
  • Data entry via voice recognition
  • CSV file import and export

3. Data Storage

All user data is stored only on the user's device. The App does not use its own external servers and does not transmit user-entered data over the internet.

4. In-App Purchases

The App offers in-app purchases (Pro upgrade) through Apple's StoreKit. The purchase process involves communication with Apple's servers, which is handled in accordance with Apple's Privacy Policy. The developer does not have access to users' payment information.

5. Third-Party Sharing

The App does not share user data with any third parties. Except for communication with Apple for in-app purchase processing, no user data is transmitted externally.

6. Voice Data Processing

The App uses Apple's Speech Framework to convert speech to text. Voice data is discarded immediately after conversion and is neither stored nor transmitted separately. Voice recognition processing is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is designed for use by teachers. Student personal information is stored only on the teacher's device. The App does not collect personal information directly from students.

8. Data Deletion

Users can delete stored data at any time within the App. Uninstalling the App will completely remove all data from the device.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated. Any changes will be communicated through App updates.
